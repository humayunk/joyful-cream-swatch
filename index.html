How to Color Match at Home Depot

  1. Ask for Behr Creamy White (YL-W03) — it's the closest off-the-shelf match
  2. Swiss Coffee (12) is another great option if YL-W03 isn't in stock — runs just slightly warmer
  3. If an exact match is needed, print this page and bring it to the paint desk
  4. Ask them to scan the large swatch below with their spectrophotometer
  5. They can custom-mix to match — a physical print works better than a phone screen
